Egg Coffee

The Bitterness Of Coffee ,The Sweetness of Egg.

Egg coffee is a traditional Vietnamese drink that has been gaining popularity around the world. It is made by mixing egg yolks, sugar, condensed milk, and coffee to create a creamy and rich beverage. The egg yolks are whisked until they become frothy and then mixed with the other ingredients. The coffee is usually made using a French press or drip coffee maker.

Egg coffee has a unique taste that is both sweet and savory. The egg yolks give it a rich and creamy texture, while the coffee provides a strong and bold flavor. It is often served hot, but can also be enjoyed cold.

Whether you are a fan of traditional Vietnamese cuisine or simply looking to try something new, egg coffee is definitely worth a try.
