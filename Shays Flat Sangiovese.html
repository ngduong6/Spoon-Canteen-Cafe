Shays Flat Sangiovese

Australian, Red, Shays Flat Sangiovese, Tournon.

A red wine produced from the Sangiovese grape, a famous grape variety from Toscana, Italy. This wine is made from ripe grape clusters and hand-harvested from Shays Flat vineyard, a garden located in the Central Victoria highlands, Australia.

A deep ruby red color and complex aromas of ripe raspberries, black cherries and spices. The taste of this wine is balanced with ripe red fruit flavors and a slight tannin, soft tannins.

Shays Flat Sangiovese wine is quite easy to drink and goes well with many types of dishes, especially Italian dishes such as pasta, pizza and red meat. It can also be enjoyed alone during family gatherings or parties.

With excellent quality and affordable prices, Shays Flat Sangiovese is a great choice for lovers of fruity and easy-drinking red wines.
